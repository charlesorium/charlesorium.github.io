学讲广府话1

Posted on 2019-07-24 In cantonese

how to learn a language that is etymologically close to my native tongue but orally far form it.

Mandarin, Wu language and Cantonese are all languages语言, not dialects方言, in the Varieties族 of Sino-Tibetan languages汉藏语系. 语言和方言是现代语言学意义上的，而非传统的雅言-方言体系中的概念。But 我老家人讲的闲话，Huai Mandarin 中的合肥小片里的合肥话，is basically seen as a dialect of Mandarin.

a tree can show this clearly::

  • Sino Tibetan languages
    • Tibetan Varieties
    • Chinese Varieties
      • Wu
      • Mandarin
        • 普通话 Standard Mandarin
        • 江淮官话Huai Mandarin
        • 中原官话……
      • Cantonese

(btw, Huai Mandarin are seen as Huai language from some languagists’ point of view, for Yangtze-Huai is geographically between 中原 and 吴越 which makes it a 过渡语言)

so… you see, Cantonese is very close to the very two languages I master, Wu and Mandarin. So I am looking for some tricks to learn Cantonese quickly.

要完成这项巨大的工程，先要打好语言要素的基础

Sounds & Tones

  1. 粤语的辅音音素很少，没有清浊对立，都是送气不送气的对立。

    粤语的元音较多，但远不如单元音语言吴语多。但粤语的元音分长短，而长音与短音并不相同。

    韵尾6个，其中无鼻音三个塞音互相对立p t k 成为入声，有鼻音三个音m n ng与ptk舌位相同，也相互对立

  2. 标准粤语有九个音调，但变调会出现更多的调值，实际上则有十几个调
