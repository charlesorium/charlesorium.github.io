巴门尼德的问询之道

Posted on 2019-09-17

2.2 问询之道(the ways of inquiry)

欢迎了巴门尼德到她居所之后，女神立即描述了如下启示，这启示是巴门尼德即将接受的：

You must needs learn all things,

both the unshaken heart of well-rounded reality

and the notions of mortals, in which there is no genuine trustworthiness.

Nonetheless these things too will you learn, how what they resolved

had actually to be, all through all pervading.
(节 1.28b-32)

这一计划中的通告，已经暗示了女神将会分两个阶段(phrase)来进行启示。女神说了些更深层次的指示和告诫之后，开始着手第一个阶段，这一阶段是对她这里很神秘地说的 “the unshaken heart of well-rounded reality” 这一天性(nature)的论证(demonstration)。她在这一阶段的启示之后，接着就谈宇宙及其构成要素的原则、本原和行动，从天堂、太阳、月亮和星星到地球及其众多生物，包括了人类自身。这第二个阶段，一种传统前苏格拉底模型下的宇宙论解释，是她这里提到的”the notions of mortals, in which there is no genuine trustworthiness”。

女神这样启示的主要动机，是“问询之道”(ways of inquiry)。在及其重要的第2节(fragment 2)，她具象化了这样两条路：

Come now, I shall tell—and convey home the tale once you haveheard—

just which ways of inquiry alone there are for understanding:

the one, that [it] is and that [it] is not not to be,

is the path of conviction, for it attends upon true reality,

but the other, that [it] is not and that [it] must not be,

this, I tell you, is a path wholly without report:

for neither could you apprehend what is not, for it is not to be accomplished,

nor could you indicate it.
(第2节)

此段是北大哲学原著选读p31(4)的英译文

第二条问询之路在这儿几乎一介绍就被否决了。女神继续回到第一条路，然后谈及其他的路，展示飘忽不定的问询之路的特征。

It is necessary to say and to think that What Is is; for it is to be,

but nothing it is not. These things I bid you ponder.

For I shall begin for you from this first way of inquiry,

then yet again from that along which mortals who know nothing

wander two-headed: for haplessness in their

breasts directs wandering understanding. They are borne along

deaf and blind at once, bedazzled, undiscriminating hordes,

who have supposed that it is and is not the same

and not the same; but the path of all these turns back on itself.

(节 6, supplementing the lacuna at the end of verse 3 with arxô and taking s’ earlier in the verse as an elision of soi, as per Nehamas 1981, 103–5; cf. the similar proposal at Cordero 1984, ch. 3, expanding parts of Cordero 1979.)

这里，女神再一次明确表达了她把启示划分为两大阶段，正如在第1节里提到的那样。比较她随后的声明，位于她从第一段中对现实的解释到第二段的宇宙论的转换：“At this point I cease for you the trustworthy account and meditation/ regarding true reality; from this point on mortal notions/ learn, listening to the deceptive order of my verses” (节8.50–2).

很清晰，女神对”真正的现实“的解释沿着 第2节中的第一个问询之路 前进。有人可能会认为，宇宙论沿着第2.5节介绍的第二条问询之路而前进，而第2节介绍的两条路似乎是唯二可以想到的问询之路。但是，第6节中展示的问询之路，那条路上游荡着有关的思想 ”who have supposed that it is and is not the same and not the same” (fr. 6.7–8a)，包含了一个存在和非存在的混合物，与在之前人们看到的问询之路“that [it] is not and that [it] must not be” (fr. 2.5)不同。第6节因此似乎在介绍第三条和其他路，一条不能被归为第2节的第二条路 的路，它其实早就被否定了。同样的存在和非存在的混合物，也在女神在第7段对巴门尼德的告诫中出现。
